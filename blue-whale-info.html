Fun Fact

A fascinating fact about the blue whale is that it produces the loudest sound of any animal. Its low-frequency vocalizations can reach volumes up to 188 decibels, which is louder than a jet engine.

habitat

Blue whales are found in oceans all around the world, although they tend to prefer cooler waters. They are known to inhabit both the northern and southern hemispheres, and they migrate seasonally to find food and suitable breeding grounds.

Diet

The primary diet of a blue whale consists of tiny shrimp-like animals called krill. These whales are filter feeders and can consume several tons of krill in a single day. To feed, they open their enormous mouths and engulf large volumes of water.
