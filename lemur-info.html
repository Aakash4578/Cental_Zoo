Fun Fact

Lemurs are unique primates found only on the island of Madagascar. They are known for their distinctive appearance, including large, round eyes and a long, furry tail.

habitat

Lemurs inhabit a variety of habitats in Madagascar, ranging from rainforests and dry deciduous forests to mangroves and spiny forests. Each lemur species has its own preferred habitats

Diet

Lemurs have diverse diets, varying from species to species. Some lemurs are primarily herbivorous, feeding on fruits, leaves, and flowers.
