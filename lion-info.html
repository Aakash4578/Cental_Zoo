habitat

Lions are found in various habitats across Africa, including grasslands, savannas, open woodlands, and scrub habitats. They are also found in a small population in the Gir Forest of India.

Diet

Lions are carnivorous apex predators, and their diet mainly consists of large ungulates such as zebras, wildebeests, and antelopes. They are skilled hunters and often work together in groups to bring down their prey.
