Fish are the oldest known vertebrates on Earth, with fossils dating back over 500 million years.

habitat

Fish inhabit various aquatic environments, including freshwater rivers, lakes, and ponds, as well as saltwater oceans and seas.

Diet

The diet of fish varies depending on the species and their habitat. Some fish are carnivorous, feeding on other smaller fish or invertebrates, while others are herbivorous, consuming algae and plants.
